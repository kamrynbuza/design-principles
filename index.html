Welcome to an all you need to know website about the principles and theories of design! The content of this website includes information about the six principles of design: focal point, contrast, balance, movement, rhythm, and unity. Along with information about the principles of design, you will find additional information about Gestalts theories of proximity, similarity, continuity, and closure.
