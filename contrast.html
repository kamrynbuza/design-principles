Contrast

In chapter 5 of "White Space is Not Your Enemy", written by Hagen & Golombisky, it states, “Contrast, as a principle, offers a great deal of flexibility. There are limitless ways to achieve it… However you do it, you need to contrast in order to avoid visual boredom.” The best way to achieve contrast, is to employ opposites of an element of design.

Contrast can be shown by using light and dark colors, horizontal and vertical lines, or even by using different types of texture. This principle of design is important because it helps elements of your web page standout and ensures everything does not just blend together.
